Galway Arts Festival

Posted by Gillian on June 10, 2016

Wild Atlantic Guest House has award winning facilities (trip advisor)

Ground-breaking new production ‘Invitation to a Journey’ fuses theatre, music and contemporary dance to create a compelling illustration of one of the greatest innovators in modern design and architecture: Eileen Gray. Bringing together four award-winning theatre companies, it’s set to be one of the most exciting and talked-about shows of the Festival.

Award-winning director Ivo van Hove directs ‘Song from Far Away', a mesmerising performance about a family homecoming. van Hove has just scooped a Tony Award for Best Director, and he's worked on a string of successful theatre productions, including Enda Walsh and David Bowie’s musical 'Lazarus', His most recent work is ‘The Crucible', currently running on Broadway and starring Saoirse Ronan.

What else to expect at this year’s Festival? The multi award–winning Galway theatre company Druid presents a new production of ‘Waiting for Godot’, Kellie Hughes invites us into the surreal world of a man facing his own morality in ‘Death at Intervals’, and Lee Hall, the Tony and Olivier award-winning genius behind Billy Elliot directs the blisteringly funny musical play Our Ladies of Perpetual Succour - catch it before it hits London
