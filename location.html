Location

Getting to The Wild Atlantic Guest House is Easy

Close to both the Motory way and The Train Station Wild Atlantic Way is easy to find

By Car

Justs 40 miles / 80 Km from Shannon airport or About 2 hours from Dublin airport. On approach to Galway city via Lough Atalia Road, turn right onto Fairgreen Road. The Guest House car-park entrance is on the left hand side, before you get to the next junction, signage is clearly visible

By Rail

Galway Train Station is located on Eyre Square, within walking distance of the Wild Atlantic Guest House. For information on routes and timetables please click on the following link: Irish Rail

By Bus/ Coach

There is a regular bus service to and from Galway to all major cities in Ireland. The Wild Atlantic Guest House is located within walking distance of both main bus stations. For further information on bus services and timetables please click on the following links:Buseirann
