Wild Atlantic Guest House Rooms

We have a range of rooms to choose from at Wild Atlantic Guest House. All of our rooms are en-suite and are equipped with flat screen TV's with cable. They also contain a personal safe, central heating, iron and ironing board, tea/coffee making facilities, complimentary toiletries, extra pillows and mini fridge. Breakfast is available to guests which includes a continental style buffet and a cooked breakfast menu served in our main dining room. Breakfast is served from 8am weekdays and 7.30am weekends. The regular check-in times are daily from 11:00am - 5:30pm. Please make the B & B aware if you plan to arrive outside of these hours.

Our single rooms contain either a double or single bed and are all ensuite.
