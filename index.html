Welcome to the
Wild Atlantic Guest House
Luxury Furnishined Rooms in the Heart of Galway City

Established since 1996, the Wild Atlantic Guest House is located in Galway City Center, the gateway to the beach and Eyre Square. The family Run Bed and Breakfast has been operated for over 20 years and offers stunning views over Galway Bay.
The Wild Atlantic Guest House is a Bed and Breakfast of great warmth and character, with its great views of Galway Bay.
There are 15 bedrooms, with Walnut Wooden floors Private Bathrooms, full-size baths all with separate walk-in Tropical Rain Showers.
The Bed and Breakfast is very close to many popular local venues so its perfect for tourists and fishing trips.
