Sight seeing Tours and Activites

The Wild Atlantic Guest House can book you on tours at a discounted rate.

All tickets can be purchased on arrival.

Connemara

A truly magical day out in typical Irish countryside- let us bring it to life and inform you of the many legends from killer sheep to lake monsters!

Cliffs Of Moher

Cliffs of Moher tours leave Galway daily, the passengers are brought to the Cliffs of Moher where they have 90 minutes of free time to enjoy the Cliffs.

Bike Tour

Allow yourself to be awe-struck by the beauty of your surroundings. Feel at home with the genuine warmth of the local hospitality. Let the salty sea breezes soothe your senses.

Aran Islands

Only an amazing 45min ferry ride could wet Your appetite on what you are going to see on The world famous Aran Islands?

Galway City Bus Tour

A very exciting bus tour of Galway City that is fun for most of the family.

Walking Tour

For those who like to stretch their legs! Discounts are available for this tour too.
